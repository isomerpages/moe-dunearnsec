<h4><strong><u>Applied Learning Module ( ApLM)</u></strong></h4>
<p>The <strong>Applied Learning Module (ApLM)</strong> aims to give students
the opportunity to gain practical, hands-on experience that allows them
to apply their knowledge in real-world settings. In Dunearn, students will
participate in at least 1 ApLM when they are in secondary 3. The ApLM is
designed with the best interests of students in mind by ensuring that their
personal growth, interests, and strengths are central to the modules offered.
This approach not only supports the development of students' portfolios
but also equipped them with skills they can showcase in their academic
and career development.</p>
<p><strong>Key Features of the ApLM:</strong>
</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>Authentic Learning Environment</strong>: Students engage in activities
that closely mimic real-world tasks, allowing them to gain insights into
various professions or industries.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Personalised Exploration</strong>: The module helps students discover
their personal interests and strengths by exposing them to a variety of
fields, from science and technology to fashion design and beauty and health
wellness.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Skill Development</strong>: Students acquire important skills
such as critical thinking, communication, collaboration, and problem-solving,
which are essential for success in both further education and in the workforce.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Industry Partnerships</strong>: ApLM typically involves collaboration
with external organisations, industry experts and higher tertiary institution.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Reflection and Growth</strong>: Students reflect on their experiences,
enhancing their self-awareness and helping them make informed decisions
about their future academic and career paths.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<h4><strong>ApLM 2024</strong></h4>
<p>Below are the ApLM our secondary 3 students participated in 2024.</p>
<p><strong>1)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Introduction to Food Innovation at Singapore Polytechnic</strong>
</p>
<p>Students were given the opportunity to immerse themselves in the world
of the food technology industry. They gained valuable knowledge in areas
such as food safety, sensory analysis, and the science behind formulating
popular food products. From crafting health-conscious, sensory-enhanced
ice cream recipe to perfecting croissants with ideal textures and flavors,
students were provided with an in-depth understanding of how innovation
and technology shape the future of food production.</p>

<p><strong>2)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Fashion Design</strong>
</p>
<p>This module provided students with the knowledge, skills, and understanding
of various aspects and processes within the fashion industry, including
fashion design, fashion accessories, fashion drawing, and fabrics. It offered
students the opportunity to explore creative approaches to addressing technical
challenges related to garment design and production. Additionally, students
were informed about various career prospects within the industry, preparing
them for successful careers in the ever-evolving world of fashion.</p>

<p><strong>3)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Aeronautics and Drone Flying Technology</strong>
</p>
<p>The Aeronautics and Drone Flying Technology course provided a comprehensive
introduction to the principles of flight and the operation of unmanned
aerial vehicles (UAVs). Students explored aerodynamics, drone components,
and flight dynamics while gaining hands-on experience in drone operation.
The course also addressed potential career prospects within the aeronautics
field, highlighting various opportunities for growth and innovation. By
the end of the module, students gained an understanding of both the theoretical
and practical aspects of aeronautics and drone technology, equipping them
for careers in this rapidly growing sector.</p>

<p><strong>5)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Fitness Sports Science</strong>
</p>
<p>This module aims to introduce students to the field of sports sciences
and to explore the scientific principles that underlie athletic performance.
Students examined topics such as biomechanics, physiology, nutrition, and
sports medicine, thereby gaining a comprehensive understanding of how the
human body functions and adapts to physical activity. Additionally, students
were informed about various career opportunities within the sports industry,
preparing them for a successful future in this dynamic and evolving field.</p>

<p><strong>6) Digital Publishing @ Republic Poly</strong>
</p>
<p>This module teaches the basic skills in visual communication design using
the software Adobe Photoshop and Adobe InDesign.</p>
<p>It introduces students to the <em>workflow and roles and responsibilities </em>required
in a creative team, software tools, and develop pre-press production skills
and processes in order to create an interactive digital publication using
typography, images, and graphical elements.</p>

projects with captivating soundscapes. Secure your spot and embark on an
exciting journey of sonic storytelling today. Get ready to make a lasting

<p><strong>8) User Experience Design and Physical Prototyping @ Republic Poly</strong>
</p>
<p>This module puts you into the role of a designer, where you will explore
ideas through sketching and apply physical prototyping techniques to create
a gaming mouse. You will learn the basics of User Experience Design and
the science of ergonomics in an open-minded creative environment. Experience
the life of a designer as you prototype your ideas, model them and present
them to your friends to get feedback through feedback sessions.</p>
