<h4><strong>Overview</strong></h4>
<p>Through the National Digital Literacy Programme (NDLP), all secondary school students will own a school-prescribed Personal Learning Device (PLD). The device will be used to facilitate an environment that encourages personalised learning.</p>
<p>Dunearn Secondary School students will be receiving their learning device (Chromebook) by the end of Term 3 2021.</p>
