<td rowspan="4">
<p><strong>MOE Financial Assistance Scheme</strong></p>
</td>
<td>
<p><strong>Eligibility:</strong></p>
</td>
<td>
<ul>
<li>Singapore Citizen</li>
<li>Meet either one of the following criteria
<ol>
<li>Gross Monthly Household Income not exceeding $2500</li>
<li>Per Capital Household Income (PCI) not exceeding $625*</li>
</ol>
</li>
</ul>
<p><em>*PCI is gross monthly household income divided by number of members in the household.</em></p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>
<p><strong>Benefits:</strong></p>
</td>
<td>
<ul>
<li>Full waiver of school fees</li>
<li>Full waiver of standard miscellaneous fees</li>
<li>Free textbooks and uniform</li>
<li>75% subsidy of national exam (100% subsidy for pupils under MCYS&rsquo; Public Assistance Scheme)</li>
</ul>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>
<p><strong>Application Procedures:</strong></p>
</td>
<td>
<ul>
<li>Approach form teachers for forms</li>
<li>Documents needed:
<ol>
<li>IC/Birth Certificate of parents/siblings</li>
<li>Proof of income of all working members in the family</li>
<li>Documentary proof of divorce, death, disability, etc</li>
<li>Proof of address for other relatives (eg. grandfather, uncle, aunt etc) if living in same household. Please photocopy their NRICs</li>
</ol>
</li>
<li>Applications can be submitted to the HOD (PCCG)</li>
</ul>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>
<p><strong>Registration Period:</strong></p>
</td>
<td>
<ul>
<li>Jan 2013</li>
<li>When there is a sudden change in family situation</li>
<li>Nov 2013 for those applying for assistance in 2014</li>
</ul>
</td>
</tr>

<td>
<p><strong>Benefits:</strong></p>
</td>
<td>
<ul>
<li>Full waiver of $5.00 school fees per month</li>
<li>Full waiver of $10.00 first tier miscellaneous fees per month</li>
<li>Free textbooks</li>
<li>Free school attire for Sec 1 and Sec 3 pupils</li>
<li>Free YearBook</li>
</ul>
<p><em>*Pupils under SAC will have to apply for Exams Fees if they need assistance with the Exams fees for N and O Level. For successful pupils, the school will give them 100% subsidy for their exams fees.</em></p>
</td>
</tr>

<p><strong>EyeWear @ South West</strong><br /><strong>(Launched in 2007)</strong></p>
</td>
<td>
<p><strong>Eligibility:</strong></p>
</td>
<td>
<ul>
<li>Family of needy student resides in South West district</li>
<li>Total household income is $2,500 or less</li>
<li>Singapore citizen or at least one immediate family member is a Singapore citizen</li>
</ul>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>
<p><strong>Benefits:</strong></p>
</td>
<td>
<ul>
<li>Fully subsidised spectacles to students from needy families.</li>
<li>Under this scheme, students may receive eyewear vouchers to redeem for complimentary customised spectacles from the three participating retail outlets &ndash; The Spectacle Hut, Optical 88 and Nanyang Optical.</li>
</ul>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>
<p><strong>Application Procedures:</strong></p>
</td>
<td>
<p>Eligible parents of student to visit their nearest Meet-the-People Sessions (MPS) to receive the eyewear vouchers for their children.</p>
<p>*&nbsp;<em>A supporting letter may be obtained from the school's General Office to bring to the MPS.</em></p>
</td>
