<p>Our Sec 3 Biology students and teachers participated in ExxonMobil Biodiversity
Conservation and Explorers Programme (BiCEP) on 8<sup>th</sup> Mar.&nbsp;</p>
<p>It is a partnership between ExxonMobile and the Lee Kong Chian Natural
History Museum, National University of Singapore.</p>
<p>In this programme, students had the opportunity to learn about Singapore
natural heritage and biodiversity and the importance of conserving our
local flora and fauna.</p>
