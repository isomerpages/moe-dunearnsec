<p>Dunearn Secondary School was awarded 3 stars in the Green Schools @ South
West 2023 programme on 13th Mar 2024.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Held by the South West CDC, this programme recognises the school’s efforts
in educating and instilling public responsibility for our environment in
children and youth throughout 2023. This is our second consecutive year
of attaining this achievement.</p>

</div>
<p>Throughout the year, the students participated actively in activities
to raise awareness of the environmental challenges faced and develop their
sense of responsibility for the environment. Students participated in litter
picking activities and planned the Essential Worker’s Appreciation Day.
They also conducted presentations on various environmental topics such
as raising awareness on the dangers of dengue and World Water Day and attended
workshops to become ambassadors of the environment.&nbsp; <em>&nbsp;</em>
</p>
