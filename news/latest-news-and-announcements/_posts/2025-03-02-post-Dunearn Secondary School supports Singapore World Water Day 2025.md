<h4><strong>Singapore World Water Day in the Month of March </strong></h4>
<p>Dunearn Secondary School Environment Champions are actively engaging our
school population on environmental awareness and working towards a sustainable
future.</p>
<p>In line with our continued efforts to show our support and to celebrate
Singapore World Water Day in March 2025, we would like to share relevant
information on water conservation measures with our communities, parents
and students.</p>
<p>You can click on this <a href="https://www.pub.gov.sg/Public/KeyInitiatives/Singapore-World-Water-Day" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">link</a> to
find out more ways or ideas that you can show your support for our national
water cause.</p>
<p>Please refer to the information below on how you can do your part to save
water and make every drop count.</p>
