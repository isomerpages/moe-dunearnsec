<p>Dear Parent/ Guardian of Sec 4NA/NT (2025) students,</p>
<p>The 2025 GCE N(A) and N(T) Examinations will be released on <strong>18 Dec 2025. </strong>We
seek your support in ensuring that your child/ward is aware of the following
details:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>Reporting Details</strong>:</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Students are to report to Dunearn Secondary School at <strong><em>1:30 p.m. on 18 Dec in the school hall.</em></strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>Attire and Grooming</strong>:</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Students are required to be dressed in full or half school uniform and
must adhere to the personal grooming guidelines outlined in the student
handbook. This will help avoid unnecessary delays in receiving their results.</p>
<p>Students who are unwell should seek medical attention and should not return
to school to collect their results.</p>
<p>If your child/ward is unable to collect their results in person, please
inform their Form Teacher so that alternative arrangements can be made.
You may also appoint a proxy to collect the results on behalf of your child/ward.
The proxy must provide the necessary documents for verification during
collection.</p>
<p>To nominate a proxy, please complete the online form at the following
link: <strong><a rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://forms.moe.edu.sg/forms/oZOz0K</a></strong>
</p>
<p>If you require assistance in appointing a proxy, please contact the school
for further guidance.</p>
