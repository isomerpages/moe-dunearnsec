<h4>CCA Objectives</h4>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>To instil amongst the band members the value of sportsmanship, fellowship
and what it&nbsp;meant to bring prestige and glory to the school.</p>
</li>
<li>

loyalty,&nbsp;teamwork and moral integrity through an emphasis on the process
