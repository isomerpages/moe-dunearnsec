<h4><strong>About Us</strong></h4>
<p>Dunearn Secondary School Concert Band, founded in 1966, is a vibrant and
dedicated team of young musicians who demonstrate passion and self-discipline
in the learning of music.</p>
<p>Under the inspiring leadership of our band director band instructors,
our band has grown in skill and unity, showcasing their passion for music
through numerous performances.</p>
<p>The band aims to inspire and entertain audiences with their dynamic performances,
while fostering a lifelong love for music among its members.</p>
<h4><strong>CCA Objectives</strong></h4>
<p>路&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; To cultivate a culture of sportsmanship
and fellowship among band members, emphasizing the importance of mutual
support and respect. Encourage students to take pride in representing the
school and to celebrate achievements together, fostering a sense of belonging.</p>
<p>路&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; To instill a strong sense of moral
integrity among band members by promoting ethical behavior, honesty, and
accountability. Encourage students to uphold these values in all aspects
of their lives, both in rehearsals and in their interactions with peers
and the community.</p>
<p>路&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; To recognise and celebrate both
individual and collective achievements, fostering a culture of reflection
and appreciation for progress. Encourage members to set personal and group
goals, and highlight the journey of growth as a vital part of their musical
and personal development</p>
<p>路&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; To provide platform for members
to learn core values, social and emotional competencies and the emerging
21<sup>st</sup> Century Competencies</p>
