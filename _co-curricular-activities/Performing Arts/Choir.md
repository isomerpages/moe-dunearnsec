<h4>Vision</h4>
<p>To nurture passionate and confident choir members who celebrate diversity
and unity through the universal language of music.</p>
<h4>Mission</h4>
<p>Our choir strives to:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Cultivate each choir member’s musical interests and vocal talents through
engaging and supportive experiences.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Build confidence and professionalism in performance, both within the school
and the wider community.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Promote appreciation and expression of our multi-racial society’s rich
cultural and musical heritage through meaningful and inclusive performances.</p>
</li>
</ul>
