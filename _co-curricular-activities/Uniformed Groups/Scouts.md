<h4>CCA Objectives</h4>
<p>Scouting in Singapore aims to contribute to the education of young people,
especially in developing:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Character values such as Respect, Resilience, Responsibility, Integrity,
Care and Harmony</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Social and cross-cultural skills such as the ability to socialise and
harmonise in teams and groups that are cross-cultural and global in nature</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Beliefs and awareness of the environmental constraints of living on this
planet, respect for nature and ruggedness in the outdoors</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>The following enablers drive the achievement of these objectives:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>The patrol system</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Learning by doing</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Core scouting activities (Camping, Outdoor Exploration / Expedition, Knotting
&amp; Pioneering, Outdoor Cooking)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Scout promise and law</p>
</li>
</ul>
