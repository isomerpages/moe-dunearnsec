<h4><strong>About Us</strong></h4>
<p><strong>CCA Objectives</strong>
</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>To develop students’ interest in the sport</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To prepare students for recreational and competition in the sport</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p><strong>Our CCA also develops the members based on the following values:</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>Integrity&nbsp;</strong>:To play according to the rules of the
sport and be a good sportsman / sportswoman</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Responsibility&nbsp;</strong>: To be responsible for all training
equipment and punctuality for training</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Resilience&nbsp;</strong>:&nbsp;To undergo physical training and
possess ability to overcome adversity</p>
</li>
</ul>
