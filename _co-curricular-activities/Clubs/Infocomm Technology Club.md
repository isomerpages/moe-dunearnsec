<h4>Infocomm Technology Club CCA Objectives</h4>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>To equip students with the relevant skills, knowledge and awareness to
problem-solve using technology in a logical and creative manner.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To motivate students to influence their peers to be discerning and responsible
ICT users in cyberspace.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To develop communication skills and be knowledgeable in content curation
and project management.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<h4>Announcement</h4>
<p>This CCA will be merged with Audio-Visual Aid Club and Photography Club
in 2026 and renamed as New Media Club. Please refer to New Media Club page
for updates from early November onwards.</p>
